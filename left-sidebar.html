Welcome to Single Origin

A website for the coffee afficianado in your life.

The Website

This website was built with the intent of being a forum for those who consider them coffee afficiandos, or home baristas, to let them compare recipes and talk grinders, two of the most important aspects to brewing at home.

  • Learn More

Grinders & Reviews

While we put the recipe functionality first and foremost, grinders and reviews for them was also a big part of the website development as well, with the intent of letting people understand what tasks each grinder is best suited for and any flaws or strong points.

  • Learn More

Recipes and Comments

When building the site, I wanted to emphasize the sharing aspect of recipes, and how often people utilize different ratios and grind sizes to make a experience uniquely their own to make something different out of the same beans another person might.

Creating forms that let people go into crazy specific detail was a big part of this, as the home barista is likely to be very specific and picky about their recipes, some even going so far as to change their water specifically.

Comments was built in alongside it to let people who have tried the recipe or seen it to let others know about it.
