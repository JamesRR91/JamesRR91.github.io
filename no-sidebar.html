Welcome to Equip

The e-commerce site for pen and paper RPGs.

Products and Reviews

This is a e-commerce website for TTRPGS, where anybody can become a buyer or seller with relative ease, to sell their own unique products, not just sellers.

The products are listed on the splash page, with each having an individual page that will contain all the reviews for the specific product, which help the consumer see the good and bad of the product.

In the coming weeks we are looking to add the cart and the search bar to the site, as well as categories to help better filter products to the right customers.
