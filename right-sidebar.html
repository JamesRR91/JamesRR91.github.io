Welcome To TidyNote

For the reminders in the right place.

Notebooks and Notes

Notebooks and Tags are the primary ways in which you interact with the actual Notes, which are used primarily to encapsulate notes as a way to see them.

Notes themselves are built with a rich-text editor, in order for our users to more specifically craft their notes with any lists or bolding/italicizing as necessary.

The Website

The goal of this website is to built out notebooks and notes in those for people to create detailed notes, including some tags to help organize the types of notes.
