The Website

We built a repository of horror movies for the general users of the site to look through in a vein similar to IMDB for them to get an idea of the horror movie in question and what to be on the lookout for in it.

We also built a personal list for people to add movies to, to treat as a movie watchlist, and let them add or remove as needed.

We added reviews and a search bar as well, to make it easy to find specific movies, and the reviews for the ability to show the general consensus of people who have watched the movie.
